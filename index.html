ZiadaTime

A simple multi-stopwatch app for iPhone.

Support

When reporting a problem, it helps to include:

· iPhone model and iOS version
· ZiadaTime version (Settings → General → iPhone Storage → ZiadaTime)
· A short description of what you did and what you expected

Privacy

ZiadaTime does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

All timer data — the timers you create, their names, and which ones are active — lives only on your device, in the app’s own storage. Nothing is sent over the network. There are no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.
